Ella signed on with IMG Models after making a style statement for the ages at the inauguration last month (in the prior year, she'd done some modeling with a smaller agency, but joining IMG was a whole new level). IMG's president, Ivan Bart, told the New York Times that he was immediately struck when he saw her at the ceremony. "Ella communicates this moment in time," he said. "There is a cheekiness and a joy she exudes."
